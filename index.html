The Working Life

I have been working since March 2nd 2016 full-time as a developer in one respect or another. Initially I began as a PHP developer working on the Totara System, the 'pay-to-play' brother of the Moodle LMS. Working in a team of just 4 developers, we created custom functionality for various clients and maintained their LMS. Totara has a fairly unique framework which, while powerful, doesn't really give too much experience which can be used elsewhere. For this reason, along with several others, I decided to move on.

I found myself working at Curve Group virtually immediately as a frontend developer. Thrown into the deep end on day one, I had to rapidly expand my knowledge on the Polymer framework and how to wrap it using Cordova to run on Point of Sale devices
